About TCF

The Citizens Foundation (TCF) is a professionally managed, non-profit organization set up in 1995 by a group of citizens who wanted to bring about positive social change through education. 25 years later, TCF is now one of Pakistan’s leading organizations in the field of education for the less privileged.

Vission

Positive Change

To remove barriers of class and privilege to make the citizens of Pakistan agents of positive change.

Mission

Education / Future

Through the power of quality education enabling moral, spiritual and intellectual enlightenment.

Jazz & TCF To Digitally Transform Over 1,600 Schools Nationwide

Jazz, the country’s leading 4G operator and internet and broadband service provider, has collaborated with The Citizens Foundation (TCF) to digitally transform 1,652 TCF schools, to benefit over 266,000 students in Pakistan and Azad Jammu and Kashmir. It will also help replace aging tablets and mobile phones that run the TCF’s School Management Application allowing principals to collect data more efficiently. According to details, the three years collaboration will introduce a blended learning curriculum, revamp of computer labs, and a digital school management system.
